About Me

High School senior

Hello, I'm Daniel Tadesse

I am a senior at Mount Rainier High School who is interested in Computer Science. I was born in Jimma, Ethiopia and moved to America when I was 6. Despite battling a language barriar, I love meeting new people and making new friends! I am currently pursing my AA Degree in Computer Science at Highline Community College Through Dual Entrolment. Eventually, I plan on attending a university and getting my Bachlors or Masters degree.

My Hobbies

Basketball

I play varsity basektball for my school as well as for AAU clubs. I have been playing basketball since 7th grade and it's my favorite hobbie. Through basketball, I've learned hard work and dedication. Basketball is a common language when meeting new people and has opened many new doors for me.

Black Student Union

I am President of my schools Black Student Union. I meet with other officers weekly to play meetings, organize events, and set up fundraisers. We have had guest speakers such as Senayet Negusse of the Seatac City Cousil, as well as organizations like Student Connection. This year we have set a new MRHS BSU record of over 60 members!

Young Executives Of Color

Selected into a 9 month Program at the University of Washinging Foster School of Buisness. I spend saturdays at YEOC to work on profesional devlopment, marketing, and case competetions. Through YEOC, I've learned importand life skills like self branding, collaboration, and networking.
